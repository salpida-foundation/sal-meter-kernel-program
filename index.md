## What This Page Is For

This page is a **routing index**, not a full explanation of the program.

If you are here, your next step should be **one of the following**:

- decide whether this repository is relevant to your lab, team, or research agenda
- verify canonical authority and public-boundary conditions
- identify a minimal, non-obligatory contribution path
- or leave without participation

All outcomes are valid.

## If You Want to Engage Without Commitment

At the current stage, engagement is still bounded.

The most realistic low-commitment routes are:

- read and evaluate the current kernel program
- ask whether your lab fits **External Layer-0**
- assess whether you are relevant to one bounded technical layer
- contribute only at the level of interpretation, validation, or uncertainty reduction
- continue observing without participating

No immediate commitment is required.
